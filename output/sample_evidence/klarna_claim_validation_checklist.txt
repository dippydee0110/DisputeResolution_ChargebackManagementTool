Document ID: DOC-KLARNA-VAL-005
Type: Klarna Claim Validation Checklist

Validation Steps:
- Confirm Klarna claim reference and purchase session.
- Verify merchant delivery and service timeline.
- Compare buyer complaint text with platform case notes.
- Validate amount split between principal and fees.

Required Evidence:
- Order confirmation and fulfillment proof.
- Message timeline between guest and host.
- Property or service condition evidence.

Decision Support:
- If merchant records fully match and no service gap exists, recommend closure.
- If conflicting records exist, keep claim in investigating state.
