Document ID: DOC-CARD-REP-002
Type: Card Representment Checklist
Owner: Airbnb Disputes Operations

Representment Packet Must Include:
- Dispute summary with reason code mapping.
- Transaction proof (authorization + settlement).
- Fulfillment proof (stay/check-in or service delivery).
- Communication evidence with timestamps.
- Policy acknowledgement signed by merchant.

Quality Controls:
- All timestamps must be in UTC and local property time.
- Guest and merchant names must match case profile records.
- Amounts must reconcile with payout and fee ledger.

Submission Rule:
- Submit packet no later than 48 hours before issuer deadline.
