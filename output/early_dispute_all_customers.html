Evidence Action Summary

The following actions are suggested for customer-facing teams based on current evidence gaps and dispute severity.

  • Critical cases: Immediately request signed delivery receipts, merchant authorization data, and customer communication logs.
  • High cases: Obtain transaction details and invoice history to strengthen representment materials.
  • Medium cases: Confirm delivery status and follow up with the customer for any unverified details.
  • Low cases: Document the case as routine and provide standard support while monitoring for escalation.
