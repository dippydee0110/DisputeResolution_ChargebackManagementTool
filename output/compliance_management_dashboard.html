Arbitration Process Overview

The compliance arbitration process ensures the merchant coordinates with card brands and payment platforms to resolve disputes fairly. This dashboard highlights the current status, internal control gaps, and recommended improvements for each external payment partner.

Step 1 • Case Intake
Document Payment Provider
Identify whether the dispute involves PayPal, Google Pay, Klarna, UPI, PIX, or another processor, and capture provider case IDs immediately.
Step 2 • Evidence Review
Assess Required Compliance Evidence
Review evidence requirements for each network and obtain transaction receipts, authorization logs, and customer consent records.
Step 3 • Escalation
Engage Payment Brand
Escalate high-value or critical disputes through the provider’s arbitration workflow and track deadlines in the case management system.
Step 4 • Resolution
Close with Improvement Actions
After decision, capture root causes and apply corrective actions to reduce future compliance risk.
