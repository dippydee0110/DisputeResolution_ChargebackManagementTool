# Metrics and Results Slide

## Title
Building a Holistic Disputes Platform: Loss Reduction + Trust Preservation

## Problem
- Chargeback operations were fragmented across lifecycle stages and payment methods.
- Evidence quality and policy timing mismatches caused avoidable losses.
- Limited proactive dispute deflection before escalation.

## Product Bet
- Unified dispute lifecycle across ingestion, representment, pre-arb, and recovery.
- Reason-code/payment-method decisioning with policy-aware controls.
- Evidence completeness and financial ledger transparency in one operator flow.

## North-Star Impact (Target)
- Net chargeback loss rate: down 15-25%
- Representment win rate: up 8-12 pts
- Pre-arbitration coverage: up 20-30 pts
- Dispute deflection before chargeback: up 10-18%
- Median time to resolution: down 20-30%

## Guardrail Metrics
- Guest attrition risk: no material increase
- Host satisfaction for resolved disputes: improve or hold
- Policy exception rate: down 30%+
- Manual review rate on complete evidence packages: down 25%+

## Segmentation Views
- By reason code
- By payment method (Card, Klarna, UPI)
- By processor
- By geography and product vertical

## Example Outcome Narrative
- Introduced reason-code and payment-method decisioning + missing-evidence prompts.
- Improved evidence package completeness before representment submission.
- Reduced avoidable losses while preserving balanced outcomes for hosts and guests.

## Slide Footer (Execution)
- Built cross-functionally with Eng, DS, Ops, Trust, and Support.
- Rolled out in staged experiments with clear kill-switches and policy controls.
