    Merchant Guidance

    Use this checklist when reviewing merchant-level early disputes:

    • Confirm merchant pattern: Multiple customers disputing the same merchant or product line may indicate a systemic issue.
    • Escalate high/critical merchants: Treat merchants with high risk aggregates as candidates for temporary hold review and deeper evidence collection.
    • Route to customer templates: Link each case to the appropriate customer-facing risk-level view for consistent messaging.
    • Track evidence status: Ensure merchant supplies authorization receipts, delivery proof, or service confirmation before filing representment.
